Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?
+

Most projects take 2-4 weeks depending on the package you choose. Starter packages typically deliver in 2-3 weeks, while Professional packages take 3-4 weeks. Enterprise projects have custom timelines based on scope.

Do I need to provide content and images?
+

We can work with whatever you have! If you have existing content and images, we'll use those. If not, we can help with professional copywriting and stock photography, or connect you with professional photographers in your area.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?
+

Absolutely! All our websites are fully responsive and look great on phones, tablets, and desktops. Mobile optimization is included in every package.

Can I update the website myself after it's built?
+

Yes! We build websites with easy-to-use content management systems. We'll provide training so you can make updates yourself, or we offer ongoing maintenance packages if you prefer us to handle updates.

What's included in the post-live monthly support?
+

Support includes bug fixes, technical assistance, and minor content updates. The cost of monthly maintenance is determined based on the complexity of your website and the level of support you require.

Do you offer hosting and domain registration?
+

Yes! We can handle domain registration and provide reliable hosting solutions, or we can work with your existing hosting provider if you prefer.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to see SEO results?
+

Most clients start seeing improved rankings within 30-60 days, with significant traffic increases by month 3-4. SEO is a long-term strategy, but we focus on quick wins early on while building sustainable growth.

Do I need a long-term contract?
+

No! While we recommend at least 6 months for best results, all our SEO packages are month-to-month. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

What's included in the monthly reports?
+

You'll receive detailed reports showing keyword rankings, organic traffic growth, new backlinks, completed optimizations, and ROI metrics. We also include actionable insights and next steps.

Can you guarantee first page rankings?
+

While no one can ethically guarantee specific rankings (Google's algorithm changes constantly), we have a 94% success rate getting our clients' target keywords to page 1 within 90 days. We focus on sustainable, white-hat strategies that work long-term.

Do you work with businesses outside Nationwide Services?
+

Yes! While we specialize in businesses nationwide, we work with clients nationwide. Our local SEO expertise translates well to any geographic market.

What if I already have a website?
+

Perfect! We can optimize your existing website. We'll perform a comprehensive audit, identify issues, and implement improvements. If your site needs major work, we can discuss a redesign with our web development team.
