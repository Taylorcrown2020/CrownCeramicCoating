Meticulous, results-driven, and built to last — Crown Ceramic Coating delivers the exact level of protection and shine your vehicle deserves.

Ceramic coating.
A professional-grade ceramic coating bonds to your clear coat to create a hard, glossy, hydrophobic shield. It locks in a deep shine, repels water and dirt, and protects against UV rays and contaminants for years — not weeks.
Paint correction.
Before any coating, we machine-polish your paint through single, two, or multi-stage correction to remove swirl marks, scratches, oxidation, and water spots — restoring a true, mirror-like finish.
Interior detailing.
A complete interior reset: steam cleaning, stain extraction, leather conditioning, odor removal, and protective coatings for every surface so your cabin looks and feels brand new.
Paint correction & polishing.
Self-healing film that shields your most vulnerable panels from rock chips and road debris. Virtually invisible, fully removable, and the ultimate defense for high-value vehicles.

A process built on
integrity & results.

After a decade working for some of the biggest names in the industry — and seeing too many shops cut corners — Crown was founded on a simple promise: do the job right, every time, with nothing hidden.

Step 1 — Inspect & decontaminate.
Every job starts with a thorough inspection and a deep, multi-stage decon wash — foam, clay, and iron removal — so we know exactly what your paint needs and start from a perfectly clean surface.
Step 2 — Paint correction.
Under professional lighting we machine-polish the paint to remove swirls, scratches, and oxidation. We measure paint depth as we go to protect your clear coat and bring back true clarity.
Step 3 — Ceramic coating.
In a clean, climate-controlled bay we apply your chosen coating panel by panel, leveling and inspecting each section for a flawless, even, glass-like finish.
Step 4 — Cure, protect & maintain.
Your coating cures, you receive a written warranty, and we set you up with a maintenance plan so that showroom shine keeps performing for years.
